# Quick Start Guide

## TL;DR

**Schedule a tab in 2 clicks:**
1. Click extension icon
2. Click any Quick Schedule button (e.g., "Tomorrow 9am")

Done! Tab closes and reopens at scheduled time.

---

## Quick Schedule Buttons

### For Today

- **In 1 hour** → Perfect for short breaks
- **In 3 hours** → Good for afternoon reminders

### For Tomorrow

- **Tomorrow 9am** → Great for morning reading
- **Tomorrow 2pm** → Ideal for afternoon tasks

### For Next Week

- **Next Monday 9am** → Weekly planning/meetings
- **Next week** → Same time, 7 days from now

---

## Example Use Cases

### Morning Reading 📰
Find article at night → Click "Tomorrow 9am" → Article reopens with morning coffee

### Meeting Prep 📄
Need docs for 2pm meeting → Click "Tomorrow 2pm" → Docs reopen right on time

### Weekly Review 📊
Review page weekly → Click "Next Monday 9am" → Consistent weekly schedule

### Short Break ⏰
Need 1 hour break → Click "In 1 hour" → Tab back after break

## Custom Time

Need a specific date/time?

1. Click the **date/time picker** (with 📅 icon)
2. Select your date and time
3. Click **"Schedule & Close Tab"**

---

## Tips

✅ **DO**: Use Quick Schedule for common times (faster!)
✅ **DO**: Use Custom Time for specific dates
✅ **DO**: Check "Scheduled Tabs" list to see all schedules
✅ **DO**: Click "Cancel" to remove a schedule

❌ **DON'T**: Try to schedule chrome:// or about: pages
❌ **DON'T**: Try to schedule past times

---

## What Happens

1. **Click button** → Tab closes immediately
2. **Get notification** → "Tab Scheduled"
3. **At scheduled time** → New window opens with your tab
4. **Get notification** → "Tab Reopened: [title]"

---

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Want faster access? Pin the extension:

1. Click puzzle icon in Chrome toolbar
2. Find "Tab Scheduler"
3. Click pin icon
4. Extension always visible!

---

## Troubleshooting

**Can't schedule a tab?**
- System tabs (chrome://) can't be scheduled
- Check if time is in the future
- Try reloading the extension

**Tab didn't reopen?**
- Check if browser was running at scheduled time
- If browser was closed, tab reopens on next start
- Check service worker console for errors

**Want to cancel?**
- Click extension icon
- Find tab in "Scheduled Tabs" list
- Click "Cancel" button
